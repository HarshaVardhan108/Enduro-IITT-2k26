Program Overview

The program is built around a Skill + Research + Application model to prepare participants for both industry and research pathways across the AI and technology ecosystem.

HT

Hands-On Training

Build skills through guided, project-based exercises from fundamentals to application.

RE

Research Exposure

Engage with cutting-edge academic and applied research in modern technical domains.

RP

Real-World Projects

Develop portfolio-ready solutions aimed at genuine technical and social problems.

IL

Interdisciplinary Learning

Connect technology, science, and human insight for broader real-world impact.

Three Specialized Learning Tracks

Participants select one track aligned with their goals, whether entering industry or pursuing research.

Algorithms for Text & Data Analytics

Week 1 focuses on algorithm design using Python. Week 2 introduces NLP and machine learning foundations.

Outcome: AI/ML base + text analytics + problem solving

Solution Tech Workshop (STW)

Build production-ready web applications using Python (Django/Flask) with ReactJS/NodeJS for real-world problem statements.

Certified by Transenigma Pvt Ltd and Prof. Pawan Goyal (CSE, IIT KGP)

Track Fee: Rs 2,000 + 18% GST

Science & Translational Research

For research-oriented students exploring AI and human understanding through deep learning, social computing, and computational psychology.

Research themes: mind, behavior, consciousness, and applied AI
